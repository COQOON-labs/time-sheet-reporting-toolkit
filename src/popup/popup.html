📊 Time Sheet Reporting Toolkit

Open any Personio page (e.g. Attendance) and click the 📊 Reporting button in the bottom-right corner to open the reporting panel.

Privacy: All captured data stays in your browser (IndexedDB) and is automatically deleted after 90 days. Only Personio time-tracking endpoints on *.personio.de / *.personio.com are recorded — nothing is ever sent to a third party.
